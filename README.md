# MIS-Tracker-DHL--Cluster-I-West-I-
The MIS Tracker for DHL Supply Chain – Cluster‑I (West‑I) is a structured management information system that tracks operational, productivity, and compliance metrics across regional sites, enabling performance monitoring, analysis, and informed decision‑making.


📊 Overview
This repository contains the Power BI MIS Tracker developed for DHL Supply Chain – Cluster‑I (West‑I). The dashboard provides a unified, structured, and interactive view of operational, financial, compliance, and governance metrics across all sites in the West‑I cluster. It supports leadership visibility, compliance tracking, and data‑driven decision‑making.

 🎯 Purpose & Objectives

Act as a single source of truth for cluster‑level MIS reporting
Enable proactive KPI, compliance, and financial monitoring
Support Monthly Reviews, Customer Reviews, Audits, and Governance forums
Track continuous improvement, safety, ESG, and employee engagement initiatives

📈 Key Dashboard Modules
The Power BI tracker covers the following major areas:

Safety Compliance – Safety audits, incidents, trainings, and closure tracking
Cost & Revenue – Budget vs actual cost and revenue performance
Cost Saving – Approved cost‑saving initiatives and benefits realized
KPI Monitoring – Productivity, SLA, OTIF, accuracy, and efficiency KPIs
Monthly Review with Customer – Performance summaries for MOR discussions
Diversity – Workforce diversity and inclusion metrics
Billing Status – Invoice generation, pending bills, disputes, and status
Automation & Innovation Submissions – Idea tracking and implementation status
CXM Survey Status – Customer experience scores and action tracking
ESG Ideas / KRA – Environmental, Social, and Governance initiatives
Resource Sharing – Manpower and resource optimization across sites
Rack Audit – Racking safety audit compliance
Kaizen – Continuous improvement initiatives and benefits
Employee Engagement Activities – Engagement programs and participation
OT Compliance (12 Daily / 40 Monthly) – Labor law overtime compliance
Agreement Status – Customer and vendor agreement validity tracking
Lease Agreement – Warehouse lease monitoring and renewals
Grampanchayat NOC – Local authority NOC availability and validity
Form 102 / 103 – Statutory labor compliance tracking
P&L Review – Site‑wise and cluster‑wise profitability analysis
Logics Update – MIS logic, calculation, and methodology updates
Document Storage – Central reference repository for audits and compliance
